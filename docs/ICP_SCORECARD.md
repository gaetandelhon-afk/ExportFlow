## 🎯 Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

### Primary ICP: Chinese B2B Exporters (OEM/ODM)

**Profile:**
- Manufacturing companies selling to international distributors
- Recurring customers (not one-shot marketplace deals)
- Complex pricing (different prices per customer)
- High order frequency with modifications

## 💬 Discovery Questions

### Pain Discovery

1. **"Walk me through what happens when a customer places an order today."**
   - Listen for: manual steps, copy-paste, Excel, back-and-forth

2. **"What happens when a customer changes their order after submission?"**
   - Listen for: time spent, errors, frustration

3. **"How do you handle different prices for different customers?"**
   - Listen for: complexity, mistakes, "discount leakage"

4. **"When was the last time an order error cost you money?"**
   - Listen for: specific examples, amounts, frequency

5. **"How many hours per week does your team spend on order processing?"**
   - Calculate: hours × cost × 4 weeks = monthly waste

### Budget & Timeline

6. **"Do you have budget allocated for improving your order process?"**
   - Direct qualification question

7. **"If we could cut your order processing time by 70%, what would that be worth to you?"**
   - ROI framing

8. **"Who else would need to approve a tool like this?"**
   - Identify decision makers

### Technical Fit

9. **"Do you use any ERP system today? Which one?"**
   - Kingdee, Yonyou = integration later
   - None = perfect for us

10. **"Do your international customers prefer email or WeChat?"**
    - Both = our sweet spot

## 🚫 Disqualification Criteria

Immediately disqualify if:

- [ ] Already using a B2B portal they're happy with
- [ ] Less than 20 orders per month
- [ ] Single pricing for all customers (no complexity)
- [ ] No budget authority / "just researching"
- [ ] Needs immediate ERP integration (complex IT project)
- [ ] Primary customers are on marketplaces (Alibaba)

---

## ✅ Fast-Track Criteria

Fast-track to pilot if:

- [ ] Score > 80
- [ ] Explicitly mentioned order errors costing money
- [ ] Willing to share an Excel price list immediately
- [ ] Named a specific budget or ROI expectation
- [ ] Decision maker on the call
- [ ] Said "we need this yesterday"
